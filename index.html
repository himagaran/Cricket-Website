Who We Are??

Since 2014, The Royal Sports Centre has created to local & international cricket enthusiasts & professional of all ages, while presenting its vast clientele a range of primary cricket equipment from reputed manufactures in the UK, India and Australia. When shopping for cricket gear at The Royal Sports Centre you are assisted with trained personnel that provide free consultation & advice on selecting the right equipment and the use and maintenance of such gear.

From cricket bats & balls, Leg guards, helmets, batting and wicket keeping gloves, cricket clothing to cricket shoes, specialized cricket bags autograph bats and stumps & other accessories required by cricketers of all age groups you are most welcome to visit us for your cricketing needs which provide you with the perfect quality.
